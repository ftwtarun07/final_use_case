Popular Openings

Ruy López

Also known as the Spanish Opening, it begins with 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bb5. A classical opening focusing on control of the center and piece development.

Sicilian Defense

Starts with 1.e4 c5. A counter-attacking opening that leads to dynamic and complex positions. Popular among aggressive players.

French Defense

Begins with 1.e4 e6. A solid and strategic opening that aims to challenge White’s center with d5 on the next move.

Queen's Gambit

Starts with 1.d4 d5 2.c4. A classic opening offering a pawn to gain control of the center. Known for its deep strategic ideas.

Kings Indian Defense

Begins with 1.d4 Nf6 2.c4 g6. A hypermodern opening where Black allows White to build a center and then attacks it.
